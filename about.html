Café Peter, founded in 2010 in Aundh, Pune, pioneered a vibrant dining experience by blending the rich aromas of fresh coffee with the irresistible appeal of gourmet donuts, creating a beloved community hub.

Conceived by Mr. Peter Chanseok Jung, a visionary entrepreneur from Korea, Café Peter—originally Café Peter Donut—evolved from a cozy café into a dynamic all-day dining destination. With a commitment to premium ingredients, creative expression, and warm hospitality, it now offers a diverse menu featuring Korean-inspired dishes, sandwiches, bagels, pastas, pizzas, and more, catering to a wide spectrum of tastes.

More than a café, Café Peter is a community where people connect, unwind, and enjoy good food in thoughtfully designed outlets exuding youthful energy. With a presence across Pune and beyond, it continues to innovate, delivering quality and customer delight one cup of coffee and one plate of food at a time.
